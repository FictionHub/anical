Frequently asked questions

Is AniCal free?

Yes. AniCal is completely free, with no signup and no ads.

Where does the anime data come from?

Airing schedules, premieres and finales come from AniList, and headlines come from Anime News Network. The data is refreshed every time you open the calendar.

What timezone are the air times shown in?

All air times are converted to your device's local timezone automatically, with live countdowns to each episode.

Can I get notified when an episode airs?

Yes. Follow a show with the bell icon to get browser alerts before each episode. On browsers that support scheduled notifications, alerts can fire even when AniCal is closed.

Can I add anime to my own calendar?

Yes. You can add individual episodes to Google Calendar or download an .ics file, or subscribe to a live feed of premieres, finales, or all episodes that updates automatically.

Can I install AniCal or use it offline?

Yes. AniCal is a progressive web app you can install on your phone or desktop, and it opens offline using your last loaded schedule.
